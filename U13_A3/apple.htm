This Webpage will tell you about the different type of iPhones you can buy and some information on them. this includes the iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Pro, iPhone 17 Pro max and the iPhone Air.

The iPhone 17, released on September 19, 2025, is Apple's latest base model smartphone. A larger 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR display is featured across the iPhone 17 lineup, with ProMotion technology (1Hz to 120Hz adaptive refresh rate) and an Always-On display. It boasts a high peak brightness of 3,000 nits for improved outdoor visibility. The phone runs on the new A19 chip and iOS 26, which enables the suite of Apple Intelligence features, including Live Translation, generative AI image creation (Genmoji), and Writing Tools. The base model receives a major camera upgrade with a dual 48MP Fusion rear system (main and ultrawide lenses), allowing for high-resolution photos and 2x optical-quality zoom. The front camera is also upgraded to an 18MP Center Stage lens with a new square sensor for smarter group selfies and improved video calls.

The iPhone 17 Pro, released on September 19, 2025, is Apple's premium flagship model. It is powered by the Apple A19 Pro chip, which features a 6-core CPU, 6-core GPU, and new Neural Accelerators for powerful AI processing. Advanced cooling technology, including a vapour chamber, ensures sustained high performance. Cameras: The device features an "ultimate pro camera system" where all three rear cameras use 48MP sensors, offering an 8x optical-quality zoom range. Main: 48MP Fusion camera with OIS and f/1.6 aperture. Ultrawide: 48MP camera with a 120° field of view and macro capability. Telephoto: 48MP periscope telephoto lens with 4x optical zoom (8x optical-quality zoom). Front: An 18MP Center Stage front camera with autofocus for improved video calls and group selfies. It features a 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion technology (adaptive 120Hz refresh rate) and an Always-On display. It has a high peak brightness of 3,000 nits for outdoor viewing.

The iPhone 17 Pro Max is Apple's largest and most advanced flagship smartphone. It is powered by the Apple A19 Pro chip, which includes a 6-core CPU and 6-core GPU with new Neural Accelerators optimized for on-device AI tasks, such as those included in iOS 26. It uses advanced vapour chamber cooling for sustained high performance. Cameras: The device features an "ultimate pro camera system" where all three rear cameras use 48MP Fusion sensors, offering an 8x optical-quality zoom range: Main: 48MP wide camera with f/1.6 aperture. Ultrawide: 48MP ultrawide camera with a 120° field of view and macro capability. Telephoto: 48MP periscope telephoto lens with 4x optical zoom (8x optical-quality zoom). Front: An 18MP Center Stage front-facing camera with autofocus for smarter group selfies and improved video calls. It features a large 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion technology (adaptive 1Hz to 120Hz refresh rate) and an Always-On display. The display has a high peak brightness of 3,000 nits, improving outdoor visibility.
