Google Pixel

This webapge will tell you about the different types of Google Phones you can choose from, this include the Google Pixel 10, Google Pixel 10 Pro, Google Pixel 10 Fold and the Google Pixel 9A.

The Google Pixel 10 is the latest flagship smartphone series from Google. It launched on August 20, 2025, and became available on August 28, 2025. The Pixel 10 features the Google Tensor G5 chip, advanced AI features powered by Gemini, and a triple-camera system. The base model of the Pixel 10 offers several key upgrades. It has the Google Tensor G5 chip, which offers a 34% faster CPU and a 60% more powerful TPU (Tensor Processing Unit). This improves on-device AI and overall performance. The Pixel 10 has a triple-rear camera system. It includes a 48 MP main camera, a 13 MP ultrawide, and a 10.8 MP telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom. It has a 6.3-inch Actua OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate. It has a high peak brightness of 3,000 nits, which improves outdoor visibility. It has integration of the Gemini AI assistant. It offers features like "Magic Cue" for suggestions, "Camera Coach" for photography tips, and "Auto Best Take" for group photos.

The Google Pixel 10 Pro is a high-end smartphone known for its advanced AI features powered by the custom Google Tensor G5 chip, professional-level camera system, and an exceptionally bright 6.3-inch Super Actua display. The phone is powered by the Google Tensor G5 octa-core processor and the Titan M2 security coprocessor.Camera System: The device has a pro triple rear camera system: Main: 50 MP wide camera with an f/1.68 aperture. Ultrawide: 48 MP ultrawide camera with a 123Â° field of view and Macro Focus. Telephoto: 48 MP telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom and up to 100x Pro Res Zoom. Front: A 42 MP front-facing camera with autofocus. Supports 4K video recording at 60fps locally. Cloud-based Video Boost can produce 8K video at 30fps. The phone features a 6.3-inch LTPO OLED Super Actua display with a 2856 x 1280 resolution. It has a 120Hz variable refresh rate and a peak brightness of 3,300 nits.

The Pixel 10 Pro Fold is a premium foldable device. It emphasizes advanced AI integration and improved durability. The device is powered by the custom Google Tensor G5 chip and the Titan M2 security coprocessor. These are optimized for on-device AI tasks and multitasking. Features include the Gemini AI assistant, "Magic Cue" for suggestions, and "Camera Coach" for photography tips. The device features dual high-brightness OLED displays. The outer screen is a 6.4-inch Actua display. The inner, foldable screen is an 8-inch Super Actua Flex display. Both have a 120Hz refresh rate and a peak brightness of up to 3,000 nits. It includes a triple-rear camera system: a 48 MP main wide camera, a 10.5 MP ultrawide lens with Macro Focus, and a 10.8 MP telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom.

The Google Pixel 9a is a mid-range smartphone that offers flagship-level performance and camera quality at a more affordable price point. The Pixel 9a is known for its battery life and AI-powered camera. Features the Google Tensor G4 chipset and the Titan M2 security coprocessor. Equipped with a dual-rear camera system: a 48 MP main camera and a 13 MP ultrawide lens. It uses Google's computational photography and AI features like Magic Editor, Best Take, and Night Sight to enhance photos. It does not have a telephoto lens. Has a 6.3-inch Actua OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate and a high peak brightness of 2,700 nits. Houses a 5,100 mAh battery, designed to last over 24 hours (or up to 100 hours with Extreme Battery Saver). It supports 23W wired fast charging and 7.5W wireless charging.
