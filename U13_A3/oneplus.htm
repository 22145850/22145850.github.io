Oneplus

This webpage will tell you about the different types of Oneplus phones you can buy as well as some information on them, this webpage will tell you about the Oneplus 13R and the Oneplus 13.

The OnePlus 13 is a high-end flagship smartphone released globally in January 2025, notable for its top-tier performance, exceptionally large battery, and competitive pricing. The phone is powered by the flagship Snapdragon® 8 Elite mobile platform (Qualcomm® Oryon™ CPU @4.32GHz and Adreno™ 830 GPU), delivering strong performance for gaming and everyday use. Cameras: Co-developed with Hasselblad, the triple-camera setup features three 50MP sensors: Main: 50MP Sony LYT-808 sensor with OIS and an f/1.6 aperture. Ultrawide: 50MP sensor with a 120° field of view. Telephoto: 50MP periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom (up to 120x digital zoom) and OIS. Front: 32MP front camera. It boasts a 6.82-inch QHD+ (3168 x 1440) 120Hz ProXDR LTPO OLED display with a very high peak brightness of 4500 nits, the first to receive a DisplayMate A++ rating.

The OnePlus 13R is a "value flagship" smartphone released in January 2025, offering high-end specifications at a mid-range price point. The OnePlus 13R aims to provide a premium experience with a few strategic compromises compared to the main OnePlus 13 model. Features the powerful Qualcomm Snapdragon® 8 Gen 3 Mobile Platform. This chip provides a significant performance boost over its predecessor and enables new AI features in the OxygenOS 15 software. The device includes a versatile triple-camera setup on the rear, a first for an 'R' series phone. It has a 50MP Sony LYT-700 main sensor with OIS, an 8MP ultrawide lens, and a new 50MP telephoto lens with 2x optical zoom for detailed portraits. It has a flat 6.78-inch 1.5K ProXDR AMOLED display with a 1-120Hz dynamic refresh rate. The screen is protected by durable Corning Gorilla Glass 7i and boasts an ultra-high peak brightness of 4500 nits for excellent outdoor visibility.
