Services

This webpage will tell you about the services that are available to offer, as well as the what you can get with the phone that you buy.

When buying a new phone you can choose a provider for your phone, that could be EE, O2, 3 or VodaPhone, as they are the main providers. You are able to get different deals depending on who you choose.

You can get coverage on your device in case of damages or broken parts, the repairs that can be done will depend on the coverage you choose from, it can vary from screen repairs to interal repairs.
