This page of the website is to show you the different types of samsung phones that you can buy, this includes the top of the range models as well as foldable/flipable models, the base versions and the budget options. The S25/S25Ultra, the Zfold7 and Zflip 7 and lastly the A56.

The Samsung Galaxy S25 is a flagship smartphone featuring a premium design, powerful processor, advanced camera system with a 50MP main sensor, and integrated Galaxy AI capabilities. Key features include a 6.2-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, the custom-made Qualcomm Snapdragon® 8 Elite for Galaxy processor, Armor Aluminium frame, Corning® Gorilla® Glass Victus® 2 protection, and IP68 water and dust resistance.

The Galaxy S25 Ultra is a high-end smartphone featuring an advanced camera system, powerful performance, and integrated Galaxy AI capabilities. Key specifications include a 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, a 200 MP wide-angle camera, and a Snapdragon® 8 Elite for Galaxy processor.

The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold7 is a premium foldable smartphone featuring a large 8-inch main screen, an expanded cover screen, and integrated Galaxy AI capabilities, making it the thinnest and lightest in the series. It boasts a professional-grade camera system with a 200MP wide-angle lens, a custom Snapdragon® 8 Elite processor for enhanced performance, and robust durability with an Armor Aluminium frame, Armor Flex Hinge, and IP48 water and dust resistance.

The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip7 is described as a compact, foldable AI smartphone with a slimmer design, a larger 4.1-inch cover screen, a 6.9-inch main display, and a 50MP camera. Key features highlighted include an Exynos 2500 processor, IP48 water and dust resistance, enhanced durability with an Armor Aluminium frame and Gorilla Glass Victus 2, and integrated Galaxy AI capabilities such as "Now Brief" and "Photo Assist".
